The full-stack application is designed to retrieve and present data from Statistics Norway 
(SSB). The application demonstrates how to leverage public API to extract 
information and perform statistical calculations. 
The choice fell on looking at the variable; Inhabitants per km2 land area, this for Molde, Lillestrøm 
(2020-2023) & Oslo. Then for the sequential years 2020,2021,2022 & 2023.
